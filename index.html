Who We Are

Strictly was founded by Cengiz Orhan, celebrated furniture designer and patron of the arts. Hosting countless events, diners, exhibitions and sacrifices, the venue itself is home to many and open to all.

Strictly makes your heart beat to the sound of street, puts the rhythm in ya feet and gets ya bum off ya seat.

The Crew

Cengiz Orhan
CEO
Studied fashion in Amsterdam, and later to work on various ventures including a successful beach club in Holland, designer at Amsterdam fashion week, TV shows in wardrobe, and has been an assistant director for movies. Cengiz soon needed more. He started Strictly Designers United and has many creative endeavors under the banner. Including designing and manufacturing industrial, tailored furniture.
